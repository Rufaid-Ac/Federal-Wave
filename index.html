About Federal Wave

Leading Electromechanical Contractor

Federal Wave Contracting Company is a leading electromechanical contracting company based in Jubail, Saudi Arabia, with expertise in handling projects and maintenance contracts related to industrial, commercial, and residential clients.

Founded as Federal Wave Specialized Contracting Est in 2017, Federal Wave Contracting Company now is led by Industrial pioneers who have considerable experience in the field of construction, Testing and commissioning, Maintenance and operation, Supply of specialized manpower, Engineers and Technicians for Instrumentation, Electrical, Mechanical, and civil works.
